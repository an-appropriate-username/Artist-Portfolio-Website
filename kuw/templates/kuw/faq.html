FAQ

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by filling out a contact form with your details and tattoo idea. I will contact you by email to confirm an appointment.
Alternatively, you can send me a message on my instagram, with your idea and availability. (Find my social media link in the footer)

How much will my tattoo cost?

Tattoo prices vary depending on the design, size, and complexity. For an accurate price estimate, please send me a message or book a consultation appointment.

How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?

Avoid alcohol and aspirin 24 hours before your appointment. Make sure to eat a good meal and stay hydrated. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area you're getting tattooed.

Can I bring my own design?

Yes, I welcome custom designs! Feel free to bring in your own design or collaborate with me to create a unique tattoo that suits your style.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Please contact me at least 48 hours in advance if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment. Deposits are non-refundable, but we can transfer them to a new appointment date within a reasonable timeframe.

Do you offer touch-ups?

Yes, I offer touch-ups for my tattoos. Please send me a message to schedule a touch-up appointment if needed.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Absolutely! You can view my portfolio on my website or social media page.
