FAQ

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by visiting our website's booking page or contacting us directly through phone or email. We recommend booking in advance as our artists' schedules fill up quickly.

How much will my tattoo cost?

Tattoo prices vary depending on the design, size, and complexity. For an accurate price estimate, please consult with our artists during your consultation appointment.

How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?

Avoid alcohol and aspirin 24 hours before your appointment. Make sure to eat a good meal and stay hydrated. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area you're getting tattooed.

Can I bring my own design?

Yes, we welcome custom designs! Feel free to bring in your own design or collaborate with our artists to create a unique tattoo that suits your style.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment. Deposits are non-refundable, but we can transfer them to a new appointment date within a reasonable timeframe.

Do you offer touch-ups?

Yes, we offer touch-ups for our tattoos. Please consult with our artists to schedule a touch-up appointment if needed.

Can I see examples of your previous work?

Absolutely! You can view our artists' portfolios on our website or social media pages to see examples of their previous work and find a style that resonates with you.
